About us

WELCOME TO EMP-LINKED

Welcome to the official website of EMP-LINKED Company. EMP-LINKED is a dynamic IT products development company based in Panaji, Goa.

The company was established as a startup in 2005 and since then it has been providing offshore software development and related services to the parent company and its associates. The company has developed a number of high profile products in the finance and education domains that are currently being used in Europe and other continents.

We believe that the judicious use of technology, together with a good design can reduce complexity, connect individuals, and provide valuable insights, all of which ultimately help businesses succeed.
